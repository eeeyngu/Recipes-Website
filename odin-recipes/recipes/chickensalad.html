Chicken Salad

Chicken salad is a very refreshing and tasty dish that anyone can enjoy, it is also an easy dish to make at home.

The main ingredients in the chicken salad are lettuce, chicken breast, banana peppers, croutons, etc.

Ingredients you’ll need to make the “Chicken salad.”

  1. 1 package spring mix
  2. 1 skinless chicken breast
  3. 1 hard boiled egg
  4. 1 cup of cucumbers
  5. 1 cup of cherry tomatoes
  6. 1/4th cup of pickled jalapenos
  7. 1/4th cup of red onion
  8. A cup of green peppers
  9. 1/4th cup of pickles
  10. 1/4th cup of black olives
  11. ½ cup of shredded cheddar and mozzarella cheese
  12. As much croutons you desire

Ingredients for the marinade.

  1. 4 tablespoons of olive oil
  2. 1 tablespoon of potomac
  3. 1 tablespoon of parsley
  4. 1 teaspoon of light brown sugar
  5. 1 teaspoon if paprika
  6. ½ teaspoon of salt

Ingredients for the dressing.

  1. ½ cup of olive oil
  2. 1/4th cup of lemon juice
  3. ½ cup of greek yogurt
  4. ½ tablespoon of za’atar
  5. ½ teaspoon of salt

    6. How to make the “Chicken Salad.”

    1. The first step is to combine marinade ingredients and brush onto the chicken.
    2. Grill the chicken until well-browned, then cut into small pieces.
    3. Prepare dressing by blending together olive oil, lemon juice, za'atar and Greek yogurt, and set aside.
    4. Place spring mix into a large salad bowl.
    5. Slice onions, hard boiled egg, and cucumber before adding to salad bowl along with remaining mix-ins.
    6. Top with shredded cheese and croutons, then drizzle generously with dressing.
    7. Toss together, and enjoy with a plastic fork for added authenticity.

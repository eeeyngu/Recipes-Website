Microwave Bread

Bread is a staple and is found at your nearest grocery store. It is sold in a recyclable plastic bag and is found near the bakery goods section.

The main ingredients found in bread are flour, water, salt, and yeast.

Ingredients you’ll need to make the “Microwave Bread.”

  1. Grocery store bread
  2. A microwave
  3. And you of course to make it

How to make “Microwave Bread”

  1. The first step is to take the bread out of bag
  2. Find a plate in your kitchen and place the bread on the plate
  3. Place your bread inside the microwave
  4. Heat the bread for 1 minute in the microwave.
  5. Once done heating, take the bread out and enjoy your microwaved bread
  6. It is crucial not to burn the bread in the microwave.
